Data Collection

Two types of data were collected during this project. 1, a table of Io storms imported from the software, Radio Jupiter pro software, which shows the probability of Io activity, and can tell one whether or not it is in beam of their telescope. This data lists a month of Io emissions with probability higher then about 15%. There are two tables present in this data set, one for February, and one for the first half of March.
